Pooch Policy

Like the fruit they are named after, Lemon Tree Hotels are fresh, cool and sparkling with zest. They are geared towards offering our guests a 'refreshingly different' experience. An endearing element of our brand is the Pooch (K9) Policy. Most of our hotels adopt at least one dog who stays on-site and performs specific roles, some of which are self-defined and inscrutable. For example, some popular roles are Top Dog, Guest Protection Services; K9 Security Squad; EA to a more senior pooch; and Wellness Associate. Our dogs are always street dogs who are in need of a good home. Typically, we adopt them as pups of the street, inoculate them and then retain them as residents of the hotel.
